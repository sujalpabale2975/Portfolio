About Me

Hello! I'm Sujal Pabale, a passionate cybersecurity student currently pursuing B.Tech in Cyber Security (3rd Year, CYSE). My journey in the digital realm is driven by a relentless curiosity to understand how systems work—and how they can be protected.

From solving Capture The Flag challenges to diving deep into digital forensics and penetration testing, I thrive on challenges that push the boundaries of security. I believe in learning by doing, and every project I undertake is a step towards mastering the art of cybersecurity.

When I'm not analyzing malware or hunting for vulnerabilities, you'll find me exploring new security tools, writing about cyber threats, or contributing to the infosec community.
